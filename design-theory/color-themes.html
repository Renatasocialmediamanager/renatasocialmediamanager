Project: Color Themes

By Renata Oliveira

Using Adobe Photoshop, I developed five distinct color themes to demonstrate how specific color relationships can be used to create visual balance and mood.

1. Monochromatic

Single Hue Harmony

This theme utilizes different values of a single hue, creating a clean, soothing, and highly cohesive look.

2. Analogous

Natural Flow

Colors that sit next to each other on the color wheel, creating a serene and comfortable design often found in nature.

3. Complementary

High Contrast

Colors opposite each other on the wheel provide the highest level of contrast, ideal for high-impact visual energy.
